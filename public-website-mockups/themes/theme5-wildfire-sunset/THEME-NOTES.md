## Visual Style

### Key Characteristics
- **Dramatic & Evocative** - Captures the emotional intensity of wildfire landscapes
- **Authentic** - Colors pulled directly from sunset/wildfire photography
- **Sophisticated** - Not cartoonish or overly bright
- **Warm & Inviting** - Despite serious subject matter

### Unique Features
1. **Smoky Overlay Animation** - Subtle shifting gradients simulate smoke/haze
2. **Ember Glow Effect** - Pulsing radial gradient mimics glowing embers
3. **Dark Section** - Full dark brown/charcoal section for contrast
4. **Sunset Gradients** - Multi-stop gradients across all sections
5. **Dramatic Shadows** - Deeper shadows than other themes for richness

## Technical Features

### Animations
- **Smoke shift:** 20s infinite ease-in-out (opacity 0.6 ‚Üí 0.9)
- **Ember glow:** 8s infinite ease-in-out (scale + translate + opacity)
- **Standard scroll:** Progressive disclosure fade/slide-ins

### Gradients
- **3-stop hero:** `#160e08 ‚Üí #8e4b27 ‚Üí #dc7e49`
- **Accent gradient:** `#dc7e49 ‚Üí #84325F` (used throughout)
