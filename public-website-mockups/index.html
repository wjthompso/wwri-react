About These Mockups

These five themes demonstrate different design approaches for the WWRI public website. Each balances professional credibility with modern visual appeal, targeting policy makers, resource managers, and homeowners (45+ demographic).

All themes include:

  • Progressive disclosure (scroll-triggered animations)
  • Tile-based alternating layouts
  • Gradient backgrounds (not solid colors)
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Placeholder content ready for Cat's videos and copy

Professional Gradient

Modern Corporate, Data-Driven

Navy and teal gradients with clean typography, generous whitespace, and subtle glassmorphism. Sharp, professional aesthetic that conveys credibility and institutional trust.

Best For: Policy makers, institutional stakeholders, grant reviewers
